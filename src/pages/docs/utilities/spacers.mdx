## [Padding and margin](#padding-and-margin)
### [How does it work](#how-does-it-work)

Using our padding and margin utilities is simple. It's _what_ you're setting, the _direction_, and the number based on our spacing map.

`{padding, p or margin, m }{ direction if not surrounding: t, b, l, r, x, y }-{ number }`

### [basic](#basic)

<Classes result="true">
  <Classesrow classname="{padding, p or margin, m }-{ 0-7 }">
    Set `padding` or `margin` from `0`-`6.5rem`.
  </Classesrow>
</Classes>

### [x y axis](#x-y-axis)

<Classes result="true">
  <Classesrow classname="{padding, p or margin, m }x-{ 0-7 }">

  </Classesrow>
  <Classesrow classname="{padding, p or margin, m }y-{ 0-7 }">

### [top bottom left right](#top-bottom-left-right)

<Classes result="true">
  <Classesrow classname="{padding, p or margin, m }{ t, b, l, r}-{ 0-7 }">

<Codemarkup>
```html
$spacer: 1rem;
$spacers: (
  0: 0,
  1: ($spacer * .25),
  2: ($spacer * .5),
  3: $spacer,
  4: ($spacer * 2),
  5: ($spacer * 3),
  6: ($spacer * 4.5),
  7: ($spacer * 6.5)
);
```
</Codemarkup>
